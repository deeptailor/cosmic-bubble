2: How

Maneuver your bubble to absorb the smaller bubbles and watch your bubble grow. Beware of the bigger bubbles, touch them and they will be eat you!

3: Levels

When you absorb the second last bubble, you move on to the next level! Remember enemy bubbles get faster at every level!
