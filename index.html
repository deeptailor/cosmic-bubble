COSMIC BUBBLE

Instructions

As the yellow bubble, your goal is to absorb smaller bubbles and grow till you can absorb the bigger bubbles. But be careful, if you touch a bubble bigger than yours, you will be absorbed and the game will end. When you absorb the second last bubble, you will move on to the next level with faster enemy bubbles. This game has infinite levels, so play till you beat your highscore!
